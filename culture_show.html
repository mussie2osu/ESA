  • Culture Show

The first annual “Night by the Red Sea” culture show was held January 25, 2025 in Ohio Union’s Performance Hall. The event sold out over 500 tickets and included vendors such as Red Sea Beauty, Kab Libey, and Haben Eri, as well as photobooth, red carpet, and art gallery.

The show involved over 50+ Eritrean students in every element of the production, showcasing our deep-rooted love for community, and pride in our national identity. Hosted by comedian and social media icon Filmon “Gergish” Yohannes, the night featured:

  • Traditional dance performance of Eritrea’s 9 ethnic groups
  • Fashion show
  • Keynote speaker Dr. Charity Martin-King
  • Guest speaker 2024 Paris Olympic Swimmer Aaron Owusu
  • Poetry
  • History of Eritrean Community in Columbus” Mini-documentary
