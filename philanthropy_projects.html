Haben-Eri

We launched a fundraiser initiative to support disabled Eritrean veterans with Haben Eri. Haben Eri is an Atlanta based 501(c)3 dedicated to supporting veterans financially, physically, and professionally. Our goal of hitting $1,000 in donations will go to support mobile clinics for those in rural areas of Eritrea.

Eritrean Hearing and Visually Impaired (EHVI) Fundraiser 2023-2024

We also launched a fundraising initiative to support the EHVI: Eritrean Hearing and Visually Impaired. Founded in 2015 in Atlanta GA, EHVI is dedicated to improving the lives of Eritreans with deafness and blindness. We partnered to commit to a goal of raising $1,500 to buy Braille displays for blind students in Eritrea. These displays are not just tools; they're pathways to education and independence. Donate today and help us spread the word!
